The First Chapter ✨

Our story started at TCS, at the SBI client location. You may call it coincidence, but I think destiny quietly smiled that day. I saw you and, without even speaking much, my heart had already made a decision.

Sometimes the best stories don't begin with a grand moment. They begin with a simple hello.

The Smile I Waited For 😊

Every day in the office, I looked for one thing—your smile. Those little eye contacts, those shy blushing moments, those tiny conversations in between work slowly became the best part of my day.

The Lunch Box of Love 🍱

Mumbai was lonely because I had no one here. Then you came into my life and changed that. You made chapati and sabji for me, and every lunch break became a small celebration. We didn't just eat together—we shared dreams, worries, jokes and hopes.

You didn't just cook food for me. You made Mumbai feel like home.

Our Railway Platform 🚉

Every evening, I walked with you to the station. Watching your train leave and making sure you reached safely became my favorite ritual. Only after that did I begin my own journey home.

"Some people wait for trains. I waited for one more smile from you."

Our Little Fights ❤️‍🩹

Sometimes our relationship was 90-10. Sometimes 50-50. Sometimes 10-90. But we never kept score. We argued, we got angry, we misunderstood each other—but we never stopped choosing each other.

You call me childish, immature and irresponsible. Maybe I still have a lot to learn. But one thing I know is that I will always keep trying to become a better man because you believed I could be one.

Bhagirathi + Alaknanda = Ganga 🌊

You are like Bhagirathi—strong and fierce. I try to be like Alaknanda—calm and patient. And when we meet, we become Ganga: peace born from two beautiful rivers flowing together.
